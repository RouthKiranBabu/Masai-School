Navigating through the registration process is easier.

Steps for the registration process:
1. Go to URL: https://demo.opencart.com/(Copy and paste in rowser).
2. My Account(Click at top right side)
3. Register(Click on it)
4. Enter First Name(eg: 1234, etc).
5. Enter Last Name(eg: 5678, etc).
6. Enter E-Mail(eg: 12345678@mail.com, etc).
7. Enter Password(length from 4 to 20).
8. Subscribe(Optional).
9. Privacy Policy(Turn On).
10. Click on Continue button.

Challenges Faced:
You can use the email account once any other has chosen the same email before.
